Atedu Math

A guide to mental math!

Step 1: Visualize

Think about doing math problems in your head. Imagine the numbers in your head, keeping track of where those numbers are going once the calculations are getting done.

Step 2: Addition & Subtraction

First start with addition and subtraction. Do it one number at a time, keeping a track of where the carries are going and making sure you keep track of the numbers of the problem.

Step 3: Multiplication

The next step will be to do both the multiplication of numbers and then the addition afterwards. The best advice I have is to keep track of the numbers after you multiply them one number at a time, leaving less time for doing the math over again in your head.

Step 4: Division

Division is the hardest thing to do in your head. Start small, then work your way up to dividing with 3-digit numbers, 4-digit numbers, using remainder, etc.
