Tip #1

Commas is one of the most common things used in making sentences. Use the image on the left to study the correct use of commas and continue to observe and practice using commas in order to build up your knowledge.

Semicolons and how to use them is a big part of the ACT test. To the left is an image on how to use them correctly. Practice using semicolons in your day-to-day life.

Tip #3

Time to write! What better practice than to use the skills that you have learned and write some stories. When you get done writing something, ask someone to read them and get their feedback on what you can do better.
