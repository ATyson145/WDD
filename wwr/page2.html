White Water Rafting: Rivers

You can't go rafting without a river! Below is a list of rivers near you!

Check 'em out!

Snake River

Difficulty: 7/10

The Snake river is great for intermediate to veteran rafters, mainly due to the difficulty of the river when approaching the snake river canyon. All other parts are pretty tame.

Salmon River

Difficulty: 5/10

The Salmon river is a great place for beginners, as there are many tame steams to start off on. This is also a good river for tourists who love to see Idaho's nature.

Selway River

Difficulty: 9/10

The Selway river is one for the most veteran of rafters. The harsh waters and the numerous amount of obstacles make it a challange for any rafter to make it to the end. Plan accordingly.
